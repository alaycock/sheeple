Our Story

Years ago, when we were both kids, we wanted to change the way sheep learned about each other throughout the flock. We knew the value of face-to-face conversation and then it struck us - why is there nothing that allows us to judge and harass other sheep, in real time, online?

We goats set out to change the way other sheep can learn about each other in the meadow. Seeple app allows us to better choose who we like and dislike before we even meet them. From farmers, to shearers, to feeders to farm hands, we should be able to judge, and be judged, at any time, anywhere.

Looking at everyone in the three ways you could possibly know someone – personally, professionally and romantically – you can provide a rating and review on everysheep you come in contact with, while allowing yourself to be reviewed.

Meet Sheeple

We are a concept that’s so awful that it’s never been done before in a green space. We will allow you to show the rest of the meadow who you are as a sheep.

Steeple lets you find other sheep and rate their worth, the quality of their wool, and the cleanliness of their hooves, as well as other sheeply qualities.

Sheep will embrace their digital reputation for access to better, flocks, feed, and farmers.

This is a positivity app, rather than leaving negative reviews of other sheep, we encourage positivity, but have no real way to ensure this. There is no way at all that this could turn into a negative experience for anyone!

Our lack of experience in the digital development realm aside, we are confident that we can use our knowledge of technical buzzwords to push this app into your hooves.

Another weird feature is that we include the a dating option, find the sheep of your dreams through Sheeple. Not sure why we include this functionality, probably because our vision is very broad and unstructured.
